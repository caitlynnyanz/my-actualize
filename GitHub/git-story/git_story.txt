Short story:
  It was a dreary spring day, not a patch of blue sky to be seen. Two best friends, 12 years in age, roamed across the countryside looking for anything to cure their boredom. After climbing through a barbed-wire fence, traversing a sea of tumbleweeds, and sneaking past a badger's den, the two girls stumbled upon something that might just do the trick: a pile of bones. Not just a small bone here and there, but a couple large enough to fit a grown man. Curiouser and curiouser they continued to search the abandoned field, looking for anything that might answer their compiling questions. And then, they saw it. Just beyond a a broken fence was a mound of dirt, seemingly fresh and disturbed. Looking between each other with a nervous contenence, they realized they only had one opiton - start digging..
